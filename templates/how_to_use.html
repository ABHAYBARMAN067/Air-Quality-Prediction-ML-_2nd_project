How to Use

To use this app:

  1. Enter the values for PM2.5, PM10, NO2, SO2, CO, and Temperature in the input fields.
  2. Click on the "Predict" button.
  3. The app will display the predicted air quality index (AQI) and its category (e.g., Good, Satisfactory, etc.).
