About Air Quality Prediction

This application predicts the air quality index (AQI) based on pollutant levels such as PM2.5, PM10, NO2, SO2, CO, and Temperature. The AQI is categorized into different levels: Good, Satisfactory, Moderate, Poor, Very Poor, and Severe.
