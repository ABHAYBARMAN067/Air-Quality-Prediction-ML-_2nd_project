About

This web app predicts the air quality category based on pollutant values. Enter values and check how safe the air is in your area!

How to Use

  1. Enter the values of PM2.5, PM10, NO2, SO2, CO and Temperature.
  2. Click on "Predict" button.
  3. Check the result and AQI category shown below.

Parameter Info

ParameterDescription
PM2.5Fine inhalable particles, dangerous for lungs
PM10Coarse dust particles
NO2Nitrogen Dioxide - from vehicles & industries
SO2Sulphur Dioxide - industrial exhaust
COCarbon Monoxide - combustion gases
TemperatureCurrent ambient temperature
